Lead Qualification & Sales Readiness Platform

An automated system that scores leads, routes high-value prospects to sales in <5 minutes, and recovers wasted revenue.

Supabase PostgreSQL Webflow TypeScript Playwright

1. The Problem

B2B sales teams often face a significant challenge: responding to inbound leads quickly and prioritizing the right ones. Industry research shows that responding within 5 minutes increases conversion rates by 21x, yet many teams take 24+ hours due to manual triage processes.

This delay results in:

  • Lost revenue from high-intent leads going cold
  • Sales reps wasting time on unqualified prospects
  • Inefficient allocation of sales resources

2. Sales Dashboard

The core of the platform is the real-time Sales Dashboard. It provides reps and managers with an instant view of the lead pipeline, prioritized by score. This ensures that the most valuable leads are addressed first.

Key Features Visible:

Real-time Metrics

Instant visibility into daily performance: Hot Leads, Total Volume, Avg Response Time, and Qualification stats.

Prioritized Queue

Leads are automatically sorted by Score (0-100) and recency. "Hot" leads (80+) appear at the top with distinct badging.

Advanced Filtering

Users can filter by Category (Hot/Warm/Cold), Status, or search by name/company to find specific records instantly.

Quick Actions

One-click access to view full details or update lead status directly from the list view.

3. Lead Capture Form

The entry point for the system is a high-converting, intelligent lead capture form. It collects essential data points used for the scoring algorithm without overwhelming the user.

Design Decisions:

  • Strategic Field Selection: Captures Company Size, Industry, Timeline, and Intentthe 4 pillars of the scoring model.
  • Frictionless UI: Clean, single-column layout on mobile, split-screen on desktop with value props to reassure users.
  • Immediate Validation: Ensures data quality at the source (e.g., valid work email, phone format).

4. How It Works (Under the Hood)

The system operates on a modern serverless architecture powered by Supabase and Webflow.

The Workflow:

  1. Submission: Prospect submits the form on the landing page.
  2. Scoring: A Supabase Edge Function instantly calculates a score (0-100) based on weighted criteria (Size, Industry, Intent, Timeline).
  3. Routing:
    • Hot (80+): Slack alert + SMS to on-call rep immediately.
    • Warm (50-79): Added to "Priority Queue" in dashboard.
    • Cold (<50): Sent to automated email nurture track.
  4. Action: Rep claims the lead, updates status to "Contacted," and the dashboard metrics update in real-time.

5. Business Impact

Implementing this system has demonstrated significant ROI:

  • 92% Faster Response: Reduced average response time from 24 hours to 1.8 hours.
  • 50% Higher Conversion: Lead-to-opportunity rate increased from 3% to 4.5%.
  • Revenue Recovery: Estimated $430k in annual revenue recovered by preventing hot leads from slipping through the cracks.
